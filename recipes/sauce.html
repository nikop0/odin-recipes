Easy Homemade Pizza Sauce

Description

This pizza sauce recipe is easy to make with tomato paste, olive oil, garlic, and herbs. No cooking is required, and it tastes delicious — just like you'd get at a pizzeria!

Ingrediends

  • 1 cup water, or as needed
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix together water, tomato paste, and olive oil in a large bowl or jar. Add garlic, oregano, basil, rosemary, salt, and pepper; mix well.
  3. For best results, let sauce stand for several hours to allow the flavors to blend. No cooking is necessary; just spread on pizza crust.
